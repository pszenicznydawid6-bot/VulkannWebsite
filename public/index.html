What is Vulkann?

Vulkann is an independent anti-cheat plugin for Minecraft servers running Bukkit or Spigot. Developed entirely by a single developer, it focuses on accuracy, low false-positive rates, and performance efficiency. Whether you run a small survival server or a competitive PvP network, Vulkann aims to detect and stop cheaters before they ruin the experience for others.
